# Caleb's Herald Angels

Tags: #deck/commander #deck/mono-white #deck/angels #deck/budget

## Commander
- [ ] [ ] `W` [[Giada, Font of Hope]] x1 | Legendary Creature - Angel

## Deck Overview
- **Format:** Commander / EDH
- **Theme/Playstyle:** Mono-white budget Angels built to keep every listed card at or under `$1` on Scryfall
- **Game Plan:** Use `[[Giada, Font of Hope]]` plus cheap ramp to jump into a steady stream of Angels, then win through flying pressure, anthem effects, blink value, and resilient top-end threats.
- **Important Note:** `[[Herald of Eternal Dawn]]` is currently above the `$1` cap on Scryfall, so this strict budget version does not include Herald in the 100. Herald is the first upgrade to add if you relax the price limit.
- **Price Note:** This list was filtered against Scryfall paper USD prices on `2026-03-18`. Card prices can move.
- **Collection Note:** This deck includes cards you may not currently own.
- **Total Cards:** 100 cards including commander

## Notes
- This is the strict budget build for the deck idea. Every listed card was checked with an exact-name Scryfall search for `usd<=1`.
- If you want to turn this back into the strongest Herald-focused version, the first card to add once you go above budget is `[[Herald of Eternal Dawn]]`.
- The curve is still Angel-heavy, but `[[Giada, Font of Hope]]`, your artifact ramp, and `[[Keeper of the Accord]]` help the deck get to its bigger threats quickly.
