# Caleb's Herald Angels

Tags: #deck/commander #deck/mono-white #deck/angels

## Commander
- [ ] [ ] `W` [[Giada, Font of Hope]] x1 | Legendary Creature - Angel

## Deck Overview
- **Format:** Commander / EDH
- **Theme/Playstyle:** Mono-white Angels built around `[[Herald of Eternal Dawn]]` as a top-end finisher
- **Game Plan:** Ramp into Angels, stack anthem and lifegain synergies, protect your board, and use Herald plus other large flyers to lock up the game.
- **Important Note:** `[[Herald of Eternal Dawn]]` is not legendary, so it cannot be the commander under normal Commander rules. This build uses `[[Giada, Font of Hope]]` as the legal commander and puts Herald in the 99.
- **Collection Note:** This deck includes cards you do not currently own.
- **Total Cards:** 100 cards including commander

## Notes
- This is a legal 100-card Commander deck built around `[[Herald of Eternal Dawn]]` as a centerpiece card, not as the commander itself.
- `[[Giada, Font of Hope]]` speeds up your Angels and makes every later Angel enter bigger, which is exactly what Herald wants.
- If you want, the next step can be a second version that uses more of the cards you already own and fewer wishlist cards.
