## Notes
- This version is now a full 5-color Commander build, which gives you access to the biggest pool of strong effects from your current library.
- Noctis is the glue card here: it gives you haste/trample pressure, +1/+1 counters, card draw, life gain, and late-game reanimation all from the command zone.
- The deck is still strongest when played like a blitz/tempo list: develop a threat, keep up interaction, and use the commander to turn spare mana into momentum.
