## Notes
- This is the cleanest "best stuff in the collection" Noctis build right now. The deck is strongest when it plays as a value-midrange commander deck instead of forcing a narrow tribe or pure counter theme.
- Your best lane is to pressure with legends, then let Noctis convert spare mana into cards and reanimation so you keep winning long games.
- `[[Akroma's Will|Blessing of the Oracle]]`, `[[Herald of Eternal Dawn]]`, `[[Reya Dawnbringer]]`, `[[Cloud, Planet's Champion]]`, `[[Sephiroth, Planet's Heir]]`, and `[[Wraith, Vicious Vigilante]]` are some of the biggest payoff cards to protect and recur.
