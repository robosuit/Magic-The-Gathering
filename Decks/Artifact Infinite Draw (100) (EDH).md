## Strategy:

**Draw & Mana Engine:**
- Ramos generates color and provides mana ramp
- Mind Stone, Mazemind Tome provide card draw
- Arcanis the Omnipotent (repeatable draw)
- Think Twice (instant-speed card draw)
- Multiple artifact creatures and mana rocks generate value

**Artifact Synergies:**
- Mindless Automaton draws cards if you have excess mana
- Adaptive Automaton lords artifact creatures
- Meteor Golem and Steel Hellkite provide recurring removal
- Darksteel Colossus is indestructible, creates resilience

**Blue Protection:**
- Dive Down, Refute, Essence Scatter protect your combo pieces
- Time Stop acts as emergency control
- Unsummon provides bounce utility

**Infinite Potential:**
- Chain mana rocks into draw spells
- With enough artifacts and blue bounce/untap effects, creates card advantage spirals
- Zulaport Cutthroat-style drain loops possible with artifact sacrifice synergies
