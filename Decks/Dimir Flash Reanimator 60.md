## Game Plan
1. Spend the early turns holding up mana for [[Opt]], [[Essence Scatter]], [[Into the Roil]], and [[Witness Protection]] so your opponent never gets a clean window.
2. Use [[Sol Ring]] to accelerate into double-spell turns, fast hold-up interaction, or an early reanimation setup.
3. Land flash or evasive threats like [[Spectral Sailor]], [[Brineborn Cutthroat]], [[Cloaked Siren]], [[Vampire Nighthawk]], and [[Alphinaud Leveilleur]] once the board is stable.
4. Use [[Vile Entomber]] plus [[Zombify]] to cheat back [[Massacre Wurm]], [[Meteor Golem]], [[Sphinx of the Final Word]], or [[Consuming Aberration]] when the game turns into a grind.

## Flex Slots
- [[Fog Bank]] was cut because it stalled combat but did not advance the flash or reanimation plan.
- [[Soul-Guide Lantern]] is the first colorless swap if graveyard decks are common in your games.
- [[Mazemind Tome]] is the next artifact I would try if you want even more grindy card advantage.
- If you want the deck cleaner and leaner, trim the last copy of [[Mischievous Mystic]] before cutting the new artifact package.
