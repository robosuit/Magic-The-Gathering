## Source
- Built from the currently tracked blue, black, and blue/black pool in [[Cards/Card Inventory]].
- Goal: play mostly at instant speed early, trade resources efficiently, then turn the corner with evasive threats or a reanimated finisher.
- This is the "surprise me" build, so I pushed it toward a weird-but-playable flash/control shell with a light graveyard package.

## Game Plan
1. Spend the early turns holding up mana for [[Opt]], [[Essence Scatter]], [[Into the Roil]], and [[Witness Protection]] so your opponent never gets a clean window.
2. Land flash or evasive threats like [[Spectral Sailor]], [[Brineborn Cutthroat]], [[Cloaked Siren]], and [[Vampire Nighthawk]] once the board is stable.
3. Use [[Vile Entomber]] plus [[Zombify]] to cheat back [[Massacre Wurm]], [[Sphinx of the Final Word]], or [[Consuming Aberration]] when the game turns into a grind.

## Flex Slots
- [[Mischievous Mystic]] and [[Vengeful Bloodwitch]] are the first tuning slots if you want the deck to get leaner.
- [[Deep Freeze]] and [[Confiscate]] are available if you want to shift farther toward hard control.
- [[Tribute to Hunger]] can replace a bounce spell if your local games punish single tall threats.
- If you want a cleaner flash package later, add more cheap counters and card draw before adding more top-end creatures.
