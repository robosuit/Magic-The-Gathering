# Minotaur Tribal (Aggressive)

## 🧬 Deck Information
- **Format:** 60-card Aggressive / Casual
- **Focus Colors:** Red (primary) + Red/Black option
- **Strategy:** Minotaur tribal with combat lords + aggressive synergy
- **Status:** Alternative Build (Tribal Focus)

---

## 📊 Strategy

This is the **tribal-focused version** of the Jund deck, maximizing Minotaur synergies:

### Core Identity
- Minotaur lords that buff the tribe
- First strike & menace from Neheb, the Worthy
- Aggressive early-mid game

### Commander (Optional)
- **Neheb, the Worthy** - gives Minotaurs first strike, forces discard

## 🎯 Tribal Synergy

### Why Pick This Over Jund Midrange

**Advantages:**
- Creatures naturally support each other
- Neheb creates hard-to-block threats
- Tribal density = consistent tribal payoffs
- Didgeridoo acceleration is format-warping

**Disadvantages:**
- Less flexible (more creature-heavy)
- Fewer removal options
- Dependent on tribal lords surviving
- Weaker late game without diverse threats

---

## 💡 Key Minotaur Interactions

### Rageblood Shaman + Trample
→ 1 damage assignment counts as lethal blocking

### Kragma Warcaller + Haste
→ Swing for surprise lethal next turn

### Felhide Petrifier + Trample
→ One-sided board wipe + damage

### Didgeridoo + Ramp
→ Turn 3 Rageblood Shaman is possible
