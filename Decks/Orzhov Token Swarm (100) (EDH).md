## Why This Version
- Keeps `Ayli, Eternal Pilgrim` as the commander and leans harder into the part of the deck that already played well: tokens, lifegain, sacrifice, and grindy board control.
- Cuts the illegal or missing cards from the previous list, especially off-color cards and phantom includes.
- Adds more closing power with `Bond of Discipline`, `Inspired Charge`, `Massacre Wurm`, `Exsanguinate`, and your stronger angel top end.
- Adds more real card flow and recovery through `Mentor of the Meek`, `Midnight Reaper`, `Vampiric Rites`, `Vile Entomber`, `Zombify`, and `March from the Tomb`.

## Key Upgrades
- `[[Felidar Retreat]]`, `[[Release the Dogs]]`, and `[[Resolute Reinforcements]]` give the token plan more actual material to work with.
- `[[Midnight Reaper]]` and `[[Vampiric Rites]]` help the deck keep going after sweepers or sacrifice turns.
- `[[Bond of Discipline]]` and `[[Inspired Charge]]` are the cleanest upgrades for the "it played well but lacked something" problem: they help the deck actually finish games.
- Premium shared ramp and five-color fixing were moved out of this list so the Ramos deck can function better. This list stays stable on a simpler mana base.
