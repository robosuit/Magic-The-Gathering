## Why This Version
Corrected to follow Commander rules: Mono-white deck around Linden's color identity. Focused purely on white lifegain and token strategies. Added more white creatures and spells from your collection for better token generation and lifegain synergy.

- **Mono-White Focus:** All cards are white or colorless to comply with EDH rules.
- **Token and Lifegain Emphasis:** Creatures like [[Resolute Reinforcements]] and [[Cat Collector]] create tokens, while [[Ajani's Pridemate]] and [[Angel of Vitality]] provide massive lifegain.
- **Spells:** Included token enablers like [[Felidar Retreat]] and [[Release the Dogs]], plus lifegain support.
