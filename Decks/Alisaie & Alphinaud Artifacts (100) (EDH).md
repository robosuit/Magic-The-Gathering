## Strategy:

**Blue Flying Lords:** Alphinaud and Alisaie are both flying creatures that reward card draw and tempo strategies. This deck leverages:
- Draw synergies through artifact mana rocks and spells
- Blue defensive suite (instant-speed removal/bounce)
- Creature-focused artifacts (Adaptive Automaton lords, equipment for evasion)
- Repetitive card draw through artifact creatures and enchantments

**Synergies:**
- Dictate of Kruphix rewards casting spells
- Extravagant Replication doubles your best artifact creatures
- Mazemind Tome provides steady card draw
- Fireshrieker + evasion creatures = card advantage
- Witness Protection and removal keep the board clear

**Infinite Draw Potential:**
- Chain minds with draw enchantments
- Mindless Automaton generates cards with mana
- Multiple artifact cost reducers allow repeated casting
